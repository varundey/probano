About Us

Due to an absence of vocational subjects in the curriculum, the Indian school education system is failing to prevent a majority of students from making uninformed and stereotypical career choices as they lack clarity while choosing a stream or profession.

ProBano offers middle school students an entertaining place to learn and get acquainted with different vocational subjects through online courses.

We want every student to make an informed choice of stream and not just go with stereotypes. Why waste time and money by pursuing something which you don't like? As Steve Jobs says 'Love what you do and Do what you love'.

We are not just career counsellors, we are much more. Our courses also provide in-depth knowledge about the chosen field. Our main aim is not to cover the syllabus but to uncover knowledge.

Every child has an aspiration. But it can be killed by the pollution spread by stereotypes, lack of information and parental or peer pressure. We nurture those aspirations so that every student can live his/her dream.
